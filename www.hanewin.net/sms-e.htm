Send short messages via ISDN

the easiest solution for sending Short Messages manually or automated via ISDN from a Windows system. Simply type:

SMS Number "your message"

or

SMS Name "your message"

With configurable, automatic selection of the SMS Center based on the number.
Supports SMS Centers with TAP or UCP protocol with X.75 or V.120 access.
